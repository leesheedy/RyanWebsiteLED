What We Do

LumiPath Elite designs and installs premium linear neon systems that blend seamlessly into modern landscape and architectural projects. Our work combines visual impact with practical, low-glare illumination for safer movement around driveways, decks, paths, and entries.

  • 6x12mm compact 24V neon flex with highly flexible routing.
  • Colour temperatures: Warm White 2700K / 3000K, Natural White 4000K, Cool White 6000K.
  • 12m no-voltage-drop run with power on one end.
  • 20m continuous run with power on both ends.
  • 20m one-side power possible with approximately 5–10% voltage drop.
  • Dot-free light output in waterproof silicone casing for clean premium lines.

Guarantee & Compliance

3-year guarantee on both standard indoor and outdoor installations.

IP67 ingress protection is guaranteed where cuts are factory resealed by our team. If modified on-site by others, IP rating cannot be guaranteed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can this be installed into both timber and composite decking?

Yes. The system is designed to route neatly into both materials and can be recessed for a clean finish.

Is it suitable for indoor and outdoor projects?

Yes. It is engineered for both environments and widely used in residential and commercial architectural applications.

What happens if strips are cut after installation?

If cut outside our factory resealing process, IP67 protection cannot be guaranteed until professionally resealed.
