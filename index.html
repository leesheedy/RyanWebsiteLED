What We Do

We design and install discreet, luxury-grade LED neon lighting for driveways, steps, decks, trims, and architectural features. Every installation is planned for aesthetics, safety, and long-term performance.

  • 6x12mm 24V flexible neon profile for compact routing and smooth light lines.
  • Available in Warm White 2700K / 3000K, Natural White 4000K, and Cool White 6000K.
  • 12m continuous run with one-end power and no voltage drop.
  • 20m continuous run with power from both ends.
  • Up to 20m one-side power with approximately 5–10% voltage drop.
